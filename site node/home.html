About

Our App id for workers which is designed to enhance their safety and efficiency while on duty. With real-time GPS tracking, supervisors can monitor the location and movements of workers or visiters on their smartphones or desktops. The app can also support IoT devices to ensure all works can stay connected.

To ensure the safety of workers, the app includes several optional features such as gyro detection, motion detection, and an emergency button for urgent situations. Location sensitivity allows supervisors to set the sensitivity level of location tracking.

SERVICES

GPS tracking

The app enables workers or visitors to track their position from their smartphone, and their supervisorscan see their position on a phone or laptop/desktop in real-time.

Location Sensitivity

The supervisor can set the sensitivity level of the location, which will be defined as in range (0-100) sensitive .The range get measured in kilometers and can be changed manually and having a limit in every persons range .

Easy to setup

The supervisor can create a room (similar to Google Meet) where police officers on duty can join using a room code. The supervisor can then track their positions and movements in real-time.

Location-based restrictions

During the setup, the supervisor can set a range of coordinates within which the worker can move. If they move outside that range, the supervisor will receive an alert.

FAQ

The app uses the gyro nad acclorometer on the one to pick up the motion. It analyzes the data using algorithms designed to differentiate between different kinds of movements.

The accelerometer sensor detects changes in movement and acceleration of the phone, and the app is programmed to interpret those changes as motion. The app can be designed to us various algorithms and thresholds to determine when to trigger an alert based on the motion data.

The app can be programmed to send alerts via various communication channels

Real-time GPS tracking relies on a GPS-enabled device, such as a smartphone or GPS tracker, to transmit location data to a central server. The server then processes the data and displays the location of the device on a map in real-time.

Devices such as smartphones, GPS trackers, and vehicle tracking systems can be used for real-time GPS tracking.

An emergency button allows workers to quickly and easily signal for help if they find themselves in a dangerous or life-threatening situation while on duty.
